I am a current undergraduate student at University of California Riverside majoring in Education and minoring in Spanish. I am a first generation student and started my educational journey at a community college and worked my way to a four year university. While at MiraCosta College I learned the importance of staying involved and making connections which has helped me accomplish a lot in my journey thus far. As an undergrad I have been heavily involved on campus by co-founding the Puente Connection organization, which focuses on helping transfer students adjust to the quarter system. I also became a peer educator for incoming transfer students, I was able to provide a workshop to provide resources and mentorship.

I have also worked with the undergraduate admissions office for over two years. Here I have gained experience advising community college students on the admissions requirements to UCR. I also had the opportunity to promote my university at college fairs. I obtained knowledge on evaluating transcripts to further advise students on meeting requirements. I was also able to share my transfer experience with prospective students while helping on campus events such as open houses. I focus on helping cohorts of students who belong to the Puente Project and affiliates. Lastly, I was able to be part of the logistics team for the UCR Puente Leadership and Writers conferences in the summer.
